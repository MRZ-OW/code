Privacy Policy — MCSR Ranked Explorer

Last updated: 2026.

MCSR Ranked Explorer ("the app") is a leaderboard browser for the public Minecraft Speedrunning Ranked (MCSR Ranked) service. Your privacy matters — this policy explains exactly what the app does and does not do with data.

Data we collect

None. The app has no user accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about you.

Network requests

The app makes outbound requests only to fetch public content for display:

  • api.mcsrranked.com — the public MCSR Ranked API, for leaderboards, player profiles and match data.
  • mc-heads.net (and equivalent fallbacks) — to display players' public Minecraft head avatars.

These requests contain only what is necessary to retrieve public content (for example a player's public username or UUID). No personal information about the app's user is included. Those third-party services have their own privacy policies.

On-device storage

To work quickly and offline, the app caches the content it downloads (leaderboards, profiles, computed splits and avatar images) locally on your device only. This cache never leaves your device and can be cleared by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app.

Children

The app is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
