## Full description (max 4000)
MCSR Ranked Explorer is a fast, beautiful companion for the Minecraft Speedrunning
Ranked (MCSR Ranked) community. Browse the live Elo ladder and the global
fastest-times board, then slice the data in ways the official site doesn't offer.

BUILD YOUR OWN LEADERBOARD
• Add and remove columns to make your own data table.
• Sort by Elo, best ranked PB, win rate, peak Elo, matches, playtime and more.
• Filter by country, rank tier (Coal → Netherite) and Elo range.

COMPUTED SPLITS (PACE PBs)
• See each player's best Nether enter, Bastion, Fortress, Blaze, Blind, Eye Spy,
  End enter and Finish times — calculated on demand from their fastest runs.
• Sort the whole board by any split to rank players by raw pace.

MATCH SUMMARIES
• Tap any match — or any split PB — to open a head-to-head summary with a
  milestone "split race", elo changes, and the seed.

PLAYER PROFILES
• Elo trajectory, season stats, best splits and recent matches for anyone —
  search any player by their exact in-game name.

BUILT FOR SPEED
• Works offline once loaded — filter and sort your cached data with no signal.
• Player faces and data are cached on device.

Built on the public MCSR Ranked API. Not affiliated with Mojang or Microsoft.
Minecraft is a trademark of Mojang Synergies AB.
